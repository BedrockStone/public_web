Austin CDL Class A / Class B driver

Compensation: $13.00 - $17.00 hourly depending on experience

Are you a bit more than a road warrior? Do you like knowing what you're hauling, and who you're hauling it to? Do you want to be home with the family or do your own thing every Sunday?

Bedrock Stone & Design is the largest retail stone distributor in central Texas, and we won't stop growing any time soon. We're looking for the best drivers in the Austin, Tx area to deliver our products to our customers. Being a driver isn't good enough though. We want someone who can be helpful and friendly to our customers, someone who is willing to help out loading when things are busy. In short, we're looking for someone with pride in what he does who wants to help make the best stone yard in Central Texas a little bit better every day he's working.

If I just described you, well then hurry up and apply! We're hiring every day!

Requirements

  • Non Smoker
  • Valid DOT medical card
  • Valid Class A or Class B Drivers License
  • Experience with Flatbed with Piggyback attached
  • Experience with a Dump Truck
  • Clean driving record
  • Willing to work Saturdays when needed
  • Super motivated
