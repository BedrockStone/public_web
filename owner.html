        About The Owner

Kerzon R. Nickens

Kerzon opened “Bedrock Stone & Design” in 2002. As a successful entrepreneur in other industries, Kerzon saw the opportunity to create a unique stone environment. His vision included offering a variety of stone not only from across America , but included stones from around the world such as Peru , Mexico , South America and China .

Designing Bedrock, Kerzon set out to create a natural stone garden that incorporated landscape as the back drop to the “Bedrock Show Room”. Customers are able to walk through
the beautiful park like setting and view all kinds of stone displays designed by Kerzon.

Bedrock is a one of a kind outdoor showroom. Each unique display was created to give customers ideas for blending colors, patterns, elevations and mixing stones together to help them with designing their project. Outdoor living has become a way of life and Kerzon continues to stay current with the demands of his customers by continually improving the stone selection, outdoor showcase and customer service.
